Privacy Policy

App: Foundry
Developer: Colligence
Last updated: April 2026

1. Information We Collect

Foundry collects the following information when you use the app:

  • Email address — used for authentication only.
  • Authentication token — stored securely on your device using the operating system keychain. Never stored in plain text.
  • Usage activity — module interactions are synced to your organization's backend for operational reporting purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to:

  • Authenticate you and maintain your session
  • Load the operational modules your organization has assigned to your role
  • Sync field data (inspection reports, inventory counts) to your organization's system

We do not sell, share, or use your data for advertising.

3. Data Storage

Authentication credentials are stored in the device's secure storage (iOS Keychain / Android Keystore). All data transmitted between the app and servers is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS.

4. Third-Party Services

Foundry uses Firebase Authentication (by Google) to manage user sign-in. Firebase's privacy policy is available at firebase.google.com/support/privacy.

5. Data Deletion

To request deletion of your account and associated data, contact us at support@colligence.in. We will process your request within 30 days.
