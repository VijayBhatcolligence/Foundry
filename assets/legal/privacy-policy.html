Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 9, 2026

Colligence ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Foundry App ("the App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our App.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Email Address — Collected during account registration and used for authentication and account management.
  • Photos — Captured via your device camera during field inspections. Photos are stored locally on your device and uploaded to our servers when you sync.
  • Barcode Scan Data — Product barcodes scanned using your device camera for product identification.
  • Inspection and Inventory Reports — Form data you submit through quality inspection and inventory checking modules.
  • Connectivity Status — Your device's online/offline status is detected locally to manage data synchronization.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate your identity and manage your account.
  • To store and synchronize field data you collect (inspections, inventory counts, photos).
  • To generate reports for your employer or organization.
  • To provide offline functionality and automatic data sync when connectivity returns.

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate the App:

  • Firebase (Google) — Used solely for email/password authentication. Firebase Privacy Policy
  • Supabase — Used for secure data storage and file hosting. Supabase Privacy Policy
  • Vercel — Used for hosting our API backend. Vercel Privacy Policy

We do not share your personal data with any other third parties.

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Your login credentials are encrypted using your device's native secure storage (iOS Keychain / Android Keystore).
  • All data transmitted between the App and our servers uses HTTPS encryption.
  • Photos and report data are stored locally on your device until synced, then securely uploaded to our cloud infrastructure.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

  • You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time through the App's Settings screen.
  • Upon account deletion, all your personal data, inspection reports, and uploaded photos will be permanently removed from our servers.
  • Locally cached data is removed when you delete your account or uninstall the App.

6. No Tracking, No Ads, No Analytics

  • The App does not use any advertising SDKs or display advertisements.
  • The App does not use any analytics or tracking SDKs.
  • The App does not track your activity across other apps or websites.
  • We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to anyone.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided personal information, we will take steps to delete such information promptly.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your account and all associated data.
  • Withdraw consent for data processing at any time.
